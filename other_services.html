Additional Services by K&G Painting

In addition to our renowned painting services, K&G Painting offers a variety of additional services to meet your needs. Our experienced team is equipped to handle pressure washing, stain and finish, and light carpentry and drywall repair.

Pressure Washing

Keep your property looking its best with our professional pressure washing services. We effectively remove dirt, grime, mold, and mildew from various surfaces, enhancing the appearance and longevity of your property.

  • Driveways and sidewalks
  • Siding and brickwork
  • Decks and patios

Stain and Finish

Our stain and finish services add beauty and protection to your wood surfaces. Whether it's your deck, fence, or interior woodwork, we provide high-quality staining and finishing that enhances durability and aesthetics.

  • Deck staining
  • Fence staining
  • Interior woodwork finishing

Light Carpentry and Drywall Repair

Our skilled craftsmen are available for light carpentry and drywall repair projects. From fixing minor damages to completing small carpentry tasks, we ensure your property is in excellent condition.

  • Drywall patching and repair
  • Baseboard and trim installation
  • Minor carpentry work
